About Erica

Hi! I'm Erica. I went to college in Virginia, but I grew up in the Pacific Northwest and now call Portland, Oregon home. I am currently a student at Epicodus where I plan to study front-end development.

Education

I graduated summa cum laude with a degree in Digital Media from Easter Mennonite University in Harrisonburg, VA. I primarily focused on illustration, design, film, and photography during my time there. My senior project focused on turing illustrations into a digital format for printing and display.

Work Experience

After graduation in 2014 I spent some time working in retail before become the Digital and Fine Arts teacher at Western Mennonite School in January of 2015. During my time there I was awarded a $500 grant from the West Salem Rotary to expand the art program. Daily tasks included:

  • Developing curriculum
  • Classroom management
  • Lesson planning
  • Fostering creativity in students

Why programming?

In college I took an intro to web design class and found how much I enjoyed creating websites trhough html and css. Since then I have explored coding on my own through online coding sites and by developing my own website. Once I decided I didn't want to pursue teaching as a career and began looking into programming schools which brought me to Epicodus.
